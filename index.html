Why Me?

I'm a DataCamp-certified Associate Data Scientist and Microsoft-certified (PL-300) Data Analyst with 7+ years of experience in fast-paced retail and FMCG environments. My journey spans managing a Sainsbury's store in Scotland — leading a team of 40, driving 24% year-on-year sales growth, and generating £945K+ monthly revenue — to executing trade marketing across 1,000+ outlets at ITC Limited in India.

Today, I combine that domain expertise with machine learning, statistical modelling, and data visualisation — building predictive models, customer segmentation pipelines, and interactive dashboards using Python, scikit-learn, SQL, and Power BI that don't just look good — they drive action.
